# Kenney Nature Kit provenance

Selected palm and rock GLBs come from [Kenney Nature Kit 1.0](https://kenney.nl/assets/nature-kit), which the official page publishes under **Creative Commons CC0 1.0**.

Official archive used on 2026-07-13:

`https://kenney.nl/media/pages/assets/nature-kit/37ac38a37b-1677698939/kenney_nature-kit.zip`

Runtime selection: `tree_palm.glb`, `tree_palmBend.glb`, `tree_palmDetailedTall.glb`, `rock_smallA.glb`, and `rock_smallB.glb`.

The selected files were passed through glTF Transform 4.4.1 with lossless deduplication/pruning and `KHR_mesh_quantization`. This also repaired the source files' non-root scene-node declarations. No Draco or Meshopt decoder is required.
