# KayKit City Builder Bits selection

Source: [KayKit: City Builder Bits](https://kaylousberg.itch.io/city-builder-bits), free version 1.0.

The repository includes only the GLTF, BIN, and shared texture files used by the City realm: eight buildings, five cars, road tiles, traffic lights, streetlights, and a small set of street props. The original `License.txt` is preserved as `LICENSE-CC0.txt`.

The assets are licensed CC0 1.0. Attribution is not required, but Kay Lousberg is credited here as the creator.
