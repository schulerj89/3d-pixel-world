The official page describes five customizable chibi characters and eleven
animations. The student character was selected as the most appropriate base
for this game's girl-focused role-play direction.

## Runtime derivation

`studentpr.glb` was copied without geometry, texture, skin, material, or
animation modification and renamed to `styloo-chibi-student.glb`. The `pr`
version uses the pack's PBR materials; the creator's separate high-brightness
emission variant was not used.
